Projects

Michael Chen

Our goal for this project was to make a multi stage adventure game using Scratch. We wanted to make a game where each stage was a new concept and harder than the last. The game consists of a multitude of animals, including bats, lions, foxes, frogs and even a witch. The mechanics include, shooting lighting and arrows, and more. I furthured my skill in Scratch by the sheer volume of the coding I was doing. Backdrops, movement and the systems. My partner did most of the documentation and helped brainstorm and work on coding. Through hard work and researching concpets we weren not taught, we were able to make a multi stage game with a final boss, health and timing system, and mini bosses throughout.
