Project: Save The Princess
Course: Computer Science Principles
Duration: 8/30/18 - 9/7/18
Objective: Make a Multistage Adventure Game
Type of Project: Group Project

Our goal for this project was to make a multi stage adventure game using Scratch that would consist of intricate puzzles and skill to complete. Before designing stage, we brainstormed countless ideas and chose the most challenging option to test our limits. We were helped by neighboring teams, who aided us in brainstorming. We then proceeded to develop the idea further, including more detailed and precise stages. Our main goal however, was to design a game where every level is a new experience and concept, totally unique and harder compared to the last. Our game consists of a multitude of sprites, including bats, lions, foxes, frogs and even a witch. The mechanics of the game include shooting arrows, lighting, and more. While in the midst of coding this project, I ran into quite a few roadblocks. Things I haven’t learned or have ever heard of. However this didn’t stop my partner and I. We delegated our work, searching the internet, watching tutorials, all to figure out how to make a small action happen, like a slight animation. Through research and learning we were able to come up with a system that counts time and health, we were able to create bosses with animations that would follow you. We made interactive gameplay where every action you do matter. My partner did most of the documentation and helped brainstorm, giving me his input and helping with the code when I was struggling. I decided to take a shot at coding. I feel like we worked to our best potential.
