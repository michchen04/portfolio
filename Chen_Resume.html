Skills

Throughout my whole life, I have felt that the common core curriculum wasn’t rigorous for me, that led me to try to learn beyond the norm. I would later find myself one or two years ahead of my peers throughout Middle and High School. In these courses, the concepts are much harder to grasp upon and become tedious, taking copious amounts to learn properly and to understand. To combat this, I have developed what I consider an open mind. I try my best to ask questions when I don’t understand, I openly communicate with my teachers to help better myself in the field, and I also try my best to convince my parents to get me a tutor if push comes to shove. I don’t give up easily and I am always in pursuit of a better me, emotionally, intellectually, physically and mentally. I constantly strive to find a better me. Through challenging myself in life, I have learned many transferable skills that range from leadership and dedication, all the way to time management. Thank you for taking the time to consider me.
